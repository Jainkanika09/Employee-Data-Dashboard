📊 Employee Data Dashboard – Power BI

Empowered Employees Drive Business Excellence

🔷 Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Employee Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI, designed to deliver actionable insights into workforce composition, salary distribution, and employee behavior.

The dashboard enables leadership teams to make data-driven HR decisions, optimize workforce planning, and enhance organizational efficiency through real-time visibility into key employee metrics.

🎯 Business Objective

To transform raw HR data into strategic intelligence, enabling organizations to:

Monitor workforce distribution and demographics
Analyze compensation structures across roles and qualifications
Identify workforce trends and potential risk areas
Support leadership with actionable, insight-driven decision-making
